About Me

My name is Lucky, and when my owners walked into the Dumb Friends League in Colorado, I barked at them until they adopted me. It's funny because unless I really want to play, I am mostly a quiet pup, but I knew I had to get their attention or I would lose my chance.

Now that I fooled them into adopting me, I behave all the time, except when I am left alone. Then ansything and everything that is left in my sight is fair game to be chewed. I know this frustrates my owners, but I can't help it when there't nothing else to do. Maybe they should take me to the dog park more.

My Adventures

My most recent adventure was just the dog park, but we hadn't been since I injured my Dad and he couldn't walk too well. So it was worth the wait, and I was happy to be back.
