The Kostelac Family

On September 7th, 2019, the Angles family and the Kostelac family joined together as one with the vow that follows:


I love you. You are my best friend.
Today I give myself to you in marriage.
I promise to encourage and inspire you, to laugh with you,
and to comfort you in times of sorrow and struggle.
I promise to love you in good times and in bad,
when life seems easy and when it seems hard,
when our love is simple, and when it is an effort.
I promise to cherish you, and to always hold you in highest regard.
These things I give to you today, and all the days of our life.



We want to thank everyone that came with us both to the ceremony and to the reception to celebrate the unification of this family in marriage.
